# OVENCOOKED / OVERBAKED
gemaakt door jalol, wimme, kien, rodrygo en gerben
---
## HOW TO PLAY, for dummies.
---
### MOVEMENT
**Walking:**
- Basic WASD / ZQSD movement
  
**Jumping**
- Press [SPACEBAR]
- Can move while in the air
  
**Dashing**
- Press [LSHIFT]
- Can dash while in the air, standing still or moving
- Slight increase in speed

### Interacting with items
**Picking up stuff**
- Press [E]
- Only works when in range, cannot pick up multiple stuff
- Press [E] again to drop something

**Trowing stuff**
- Press [F] when holding something

**Putting in oven**
- Press [E] in front of oven
- Press [T] to put in the oven

### Game objectives
**Getting points**
- Complete orders by handing the correct items to NPCs (press [E])

### Recipes
**Brood**
- Bloem + Water
  
**Croissant**
- Bloem + Boter + Melk

**Taart**
- Bloem + Suiker + 2x Ei + Melk

**Koffiekoek**
- Water + Suiker + Boter
